About I AM: Becoming

I AM: Becoming is more than a movement — it is a spiritual awakening, a journey of reclaiming personal power and divine purpose. It is a call to those who are ready to become the architects of their own lives by aligning thought, intention, and action.

Our Mission

To awaken the world to the truth that each individual is the creator of their own becoming — by mastering the mind, guarding their emotions, and walking in calm, focused power.

Guiding Principles

  • Self-Control is Strength – Mastering our impulses builds our foundation.
  • Right Thinking is Mastery – Thoughts shape our reality.
  • Calmness is Power – Stillness carries the greatest influence.

The Power of Three

We believe in the divine triad: Feeling. Intention. Attention. When these are focused toward one purpose, they unlock spiritual alignment, clarity, and transformation.

Symbolism & Colors

Crown: Divine royalty and self-worth

Fire: Transformation, passion, and purpose

Water: Flow, calm, and clarity

Our palette: Scarlet, Purple, Blue — together forming Violet, the color of spiritual royalty, healing, and transcendence.
